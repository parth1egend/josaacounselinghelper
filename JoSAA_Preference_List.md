## Notes & Strategy
- **CSE caveat:** Core CSE at the older IITs (Bombay, Delhi, Madras, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Roorkee) closes far below 2229. CSE only becomes reachable at the newer IITs (Ropar, Jodhpur, Mandi, Patna, BHU, etc.) — see the ✅ rows.
- **Filling choices:** A common JoSAA strategy is to list programs in exactly this order (best/lowest closing rank first), continuing well past your rank as a safety buffer, since cutoffs shift year to year.
- **Borderline (⚠️):** Programs whose closing rank is within ~150 of your rank can flip either way in a given year — treat them as risky but worth listing.
- This list covers **IITs only**. NITs/IIITs/GFTIs use a separate (JEE Main) rank and are not in this dataset.
